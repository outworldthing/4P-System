The program is a conditional cash grant of the DSWD that gives financial support to the poorest of the poor in the Philippines.

The aim of this program is to help reduce the poverty rate of the Philippines. The 4P’s hopes to break the intergenerational poverty cycle by investing in the health and education of poor families

The 4P’s operates in all the regions in the Philippines. There are two types of cash grants that are given to households in a monthly basis.
